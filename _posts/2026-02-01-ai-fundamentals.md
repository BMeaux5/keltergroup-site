Everyone is so excited about AI they can't stop thinking and talking about it. But they are so afraid of missing the boat on implementation, they are trying to implement without a real solution in mind.

It's simple. AI doesn't change the fundamentals of good business strategy.

Implementing AI into chaos just accelerates chaos. Implementing AI into a well-defined problem creates value.

If you implement AI because you identified a use case where it will speed up existing processes, or make new ones possible, you'll benefit. You need to put the problem first.

Things haven't changed as much as you think. You have simply gained a new and very powerful tool to help.

This is where Operational Intelligence comes in, combining traditional Operational Excellence, Business Information, and the transformative capabilities of Artificial Intelligence. It's simple in approach. You just need a framework for disciplined implementation.

The opportunity isn't in chasing AI. It's in applying AI strategically to the challenges you've already identified.
